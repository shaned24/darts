    Objective
    Race from the selected starting score to exactly zero.
    Legs
    A leg win adds one to the match score. First player to the selected leg target wins the match.
    Double out
    The winning dart must land in a double segment or the bullseye.
    Bust
    A turn busts if it drops below zero, leaves one in double out, or reaches zero without a legal finish.
    Turn limit
    Three darts can be entered per turn from the board, outside-board misses, or manual entry.
